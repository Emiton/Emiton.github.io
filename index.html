Although I love writing code, I see the technology as an accessory for problem solving. I love creating value add systems which are resilient and promote growth for several different classes of people. I know this sounds a bit abstract, but whether I am putting together a community meetup or designing a high throughput data streaming service, the goal is still the same: To empower people and to create an environment which promotes success by way of effectively incentivizing users and creators alike. In a distributed system, this can be influenced by architecture design and making developer productivity a first class citizen during discussions.

My background includes a BS in Mechanical Engineering, a MS in Computer Science, data infrastructure at Twitter, mobile applications for the state of Massachusetts, robotics infrastructure at Amazon Robotics, full stack engineering for Amazon Go, real estate management and investing, small business management, and community engagement.
